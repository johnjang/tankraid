Your Explorer doesn't support canvas, you don't have html5.

Move with W,A,S,D. Click to SHOOT, press 1~4 to change weapon type

Weapon Information:

1: explodes on location. slow. Infinite ammo

2: explodes on location. fast. limited ammo

3: shoots linearly. fast. limited ammo

4: shoots linearly all the way. slow. limited ammo
